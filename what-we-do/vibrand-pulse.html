Vibrand Pulse: taking the social media Pulse of your brand every month

Vibrand Pulse is our brand new social media offering. While Vibrand Opinion offers deep social media research and insight, it is expensive and time consuming. And somewhat confusing. Pulse is simple, affordable and very very useful.

We work on Crimson Hexagon, the best (and most expensive!) social media analytics platform in the world. Vibrand Pulse uses the power of Crimson Hexagon to offer you meaningful brand oriented reporting on a monthly basis – by brand, by country, anywhere in the world. On your brands, your competitor brands – on any brand really!

Buy a minimum package of 6 months, and this will cost you R10 000 per brand per country per month. Discounts on volume available! We will also throw in a yearlong retrospective of your brand at no extra cost.

You might be wondering what you will get for you money – well, we have put together something to show you exactly what you will get.

We chose Heineken. So if you’re from Heineken here’s a free report on your brand. If you’re not, this is what a report on your brand will look like. Bear in mind that this isn’t a straight search term based report – our analysts put in the work upfront to make sure that all search terms relevant to your rand are included, and our Boolean search terms construct allow for very accurate data collection. No spurious mentions, no irrelevant asides bumping up your volume.

We are also able to analyse your twitter account for you. Here is the report for Heinekens twitter account for xxx and yyy. If you’re from Heineken, well it’s a pleasure!

You’ll see that you will, get all of the following:

  • Volume per month, benchmarked against the last year average and against the previous years month
  • Sentiment analysis per month, benchmarked against the last year average and against the previous years month
  • Gender and age analysis
  • Geography analysis
  • Word clouds
  • Topic Waves
  • Emotional analysis of the months traffic

Well, we have the answers there too – you can request additional information on almost anything you notice, and we will find it for you. This does of course cost extra – but no that much all things considered.

Well, we can provide the answers there too.
